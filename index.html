About The Clinic

A dermatology practice built on patience.

Narayan Skin Clinic was founded on a simple idea — every skin concern deserves a full explanation, not a five-minute prescription. Dr. Ketan Kacha has built a 5.0-rated practice in Bhakti Nagar, Rajkot by treating each patient's skin, and their questions, with equal seriousness.

Patients frequently mention that Dr. Kacha "understands everything and does not rush" — a reputation earned appointment by appointment, across hundreds of laser, acne, and mole-removal cases.

The clinic is open six days a week with both morning and evening hours, making it accessible for working professionals across University Road, Trishul Chowk, and greater Rajkot.
